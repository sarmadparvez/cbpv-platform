Welcome to Crowd-Based Prototype Validation Platform

You have Developer and Crowdworker roles.

  • You can create Prototypes as a Developer to get feedback on them.
  • You can also give feedback on other Prototypes as a Crowdworker.

To start creating your Prototypes, go to Projects from the sidebar. Create a Project and start a new Task iteration in it.

To give feedback on Prototypes, go to Evaluate from the sidebar and open the Task you want to evaluate.

Note: You cannot give feedbacks yet. This feature is under development.
